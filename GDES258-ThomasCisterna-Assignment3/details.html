How Does Cross-Odor Work?

STEP 1

Let’s face it, not all courts are created equal. Before you check into the game, use the traction spray. It helps repel dust and optimizes grip while playing on court surfaces.
For best results, spray three inches away from the sole. Cover the entire surface area of the shoe's sole.
And don't forget... Always spray upright!


STEP 2

WAIT!!! Don’t jump into the game quite yet.
You’re going to want to work the formula into the sole of the shoe by gently scuffing the court several times. Once you feel the liquid dry, you’re good to ball.
Pro tip: Keep a towel around in case of excess liquid near the bench area.


STEP 3

Afterwards, you have the choice to keep your shoes smelling fresh with our deodorizer OR eliminate germs and stay safe with our sanitizer. Depending on which prodcut you've purchased, spray the ORANGE END in your shoes (deodorizer) or on your hands (sanitizer).
Pro tip: Reapply Step 1 and 3 as needed
